About Your Coach!

Blake Bolton

Born in Paris, Texas in 1986. Started playing soccer at age 4 at kiwanis. Moved to Tyler, Texas when I was 10. Joined East Texas Spirit soccer club at age 12. Played competitive youth soccer in the Coke Cola Classic league in Dallas, Texas. I was a 4 year letterman in highschool. I played 1 season of college at Lon Morris Junion College.
